Getting Started

How do I import content?

Paste any article URL or YouTube link into the import box, or copy-paste text directly. LearnBetter processes your content in seconds.

What types of content does LearnBetter support?

We support web articles, YouTube videos, PDFs, and plain text. More formats are coming soon.

Is there a free plan?

Yes — you can get started for free with up to 5 imports per month. Upgrade to Pro for unlimited imports and advanced features.

Learning & Practice

What is Bloom's Taxonomy and why does it matter?

Bloom's Taxonomy is a framework that categorizes thinking into six levels — from basic recall all the way up to creating and evaluating. By setting your level, you ensure every practice session challenges you appropriately.

How does spaced repetition work in LearnBetter?

Our system tracks which concepts you've mastered and which need review. It schedules flashbacks at the optimal time before you'd naturally forget — so you remember more with less effort.

Can I re-practice content I've already completed?

Absolutely. Visit your Learning Garden at any time to revisit any concept or restart a full practice session.

Account & Billing

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel anytime from your Account Settings under Billing. Your access continues until the end of your current billing period.

Do you offer student discounts?

Yes — email us at hello@learnbetter.app with your .edu address for 50% off any plan.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Account Settings → Privacy → Delete Account. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.
