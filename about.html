About LearnBetter

We believe everyone deserves to learn as effectively as the best learners in the world.

Our Mission

LearnBetter exists to close the gap between consuming information and actually owning it. Most people read, watch, and listen to incredible content every day — and forget nearly all of it within a week. We think that's a solvable problem.

What We Build

We combine the science of spaced repetition, active recall, and Bloom's Taxonomy into one seamless experience. Paste in any article or video, and LearnBetter instantly generates a personalized path of 40+ questions calibrated to the thinking level you want to reach.

Our Values

  • Science first — every feature is grounded in cognitive research
  • Learner agency — you set the goal, we build the path
  • Radical honesty — we show you exactly what you know and what you don't
  • Simplicity — powerful tools that feel effortless
