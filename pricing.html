Frequently asked questions

Can I switch plans anytime?

Yes. Upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time. Your billing adjusts immediately, pro-rated to the day.

Is there a student discount?

Yes — students get 50% off Pro with a verified .edu email address. Just sign up and we'll apply it automatically.

What payment methods do you accept?

All major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), PayPal, and bank transfer for annual enterprise plans.

Is my data safe?

Your data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We are SOC 2 Type II compliant and never sell your personal data.

How does the free plan work?

The free plan is genuinely free — no trial period, no credit card required. Use it as long as you like. Upgrade only when you hit the limits.

What happens to my data if I cancel?

Your concepts and progress are yours. We give you 90 days to export your full data in standard formats after cancellation.
